Socrates

First Moral Philosopher

The ancient Greek philosopher Socrates taught philosophy in the city of Athens, and played a significant role in the civil life of the city during a tumultuous time. Socrates is best-known through the writings of his student, Plato and for the question and answer method of philosophical discourse, called the Socratic method.
